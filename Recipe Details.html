Spring Rolls
Mozzarella Sticks
ingredients ingredients ingredients
  • 1 French baguette, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, halved
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: mozzarella or parmesan
  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce or cabbage
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1/2 cup cooked vermicelli noodles
  • 1/2 cup cooked shrimp or tofu (optional)
  • Fresh mint and cilantro leaves
  • Dipping sauce: hoisin-peanut or sweet chili
  • 12 mozzarella sticks or string cheese, halved
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (Italian seasoned)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Marinara sauce (for dipping)
Instructions Instructions Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet.
  2. Brush each slice with olive oil and toast in the oven for 5–7 minutes until golden.
  3. Rub the toasted bread with halved garlic cloves.
  4. In a bowl, mix diced tomatoes, basil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spoon the tomato mixture over each toast and serve immediately. Optional: Top with shredded mozzarella or parmesan.
  1. Soak a rice paper wrapper in warm water for 10–15 seconds until soft.
  2. Lay it flat on a clean surface and place lettuce, noodles, veggies, herbs, and protein in the center.
  3. Fold the sides inward, then roll tightly from the bottom like a burrito.
  4. Repeat with remaining wrappers and ingredients.
  5. Serve with your choice of dipping sauce.
  1. Freeze mozzarella sticks for 30 minutes to prevent melting during frying.
  2. Set up a breading station: flour in one bowl, beaten eggs in another, and breadcrumbs mixed with seasonings in the third.
  3. Coat each cheese stick in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs. Repeat egg and breadcrumb coating for extra crispiness.
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry sticks in batches for 1–2 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with marinara sauce.

Uramaki Sushi
Mini Quiches
ingredients ingredients ingredients
  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Paprika or chives (for garnish)
  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, julienned
  • 6 imitation crab sticks or fresh crab
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger (for serving)
  • 12 mini tart shells or 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup milk or heavy cream
  • ½ cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your choice)
  • ¼ cup cooked bacon bits or sautéed vegetables (e.g., spinach, onions, mushrooms)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Butter or oil for greasing
Instructions Instructions Instructions
  1. Place eggs in a pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Transfer eggs to an ice bath, peel when cooled, and slice in half lengthwise.
  3. Scoop out yolks into a bowl and mash them with mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  4. Spoon or pipe the filling back into the egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika or chives and chill before serving.
  1. Prepare sushi rice and let it cool. Mix with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Lay a sheet of plastic wrap over a bamboo sushi mat. Place a nori sheet on it, then spread rice evenly on the nori.
  3. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the rice, then carefully flip the nori so the rice is underneath.
  4. Add fillings (e.g., crab sticks, cucumber, avocado) in a line across the center of the nori.
  5. Use the mat to tightly roll the sushi, sealing the edge with water.
  6. Slice into even pieces with a wet, sharp knife and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a mini muffin pan or use mini tart shells.
  2. In a skillet, sauté onions, garlic, and optional fillings like spinach, mushrooms, or bacon until softened.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk or cream, salt, pepper, and shredded cheese.
  4. Fill each mini crust halfway with sautéed fillings, then pour in the egg mixture.
  5. Bake for 20–25 minutes or until the quiches are golden and set.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving warm or at room temperature.
Waffles
Quiche Lorraine
Bagel with Cream Cheese
ingredients ingredients ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: fresh fruits, whipped cream, syrup (for topping)
  • 1 9-inch pie crust (pre-baked)
  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped and cooked
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Gruyère or Swiss cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ tablespoons sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water (divided)
  • 3 ½ cups bread flour (plus extra for kneading)
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • For boiling: 2 tablespoons sugar or honey (optional, for water bath)
  • Water for boiling
  • Optional toppings: Sesame seeds, poppy seeds, everything seasoning, coarse salt
  • 2 bagels (plain or flavored)
  • ½ cup cream cheese (plain or flavored)
  • Optional toppings: smoked salmon, sliced tomato, cucumber, capers, red onion, fresh herbs
Instructions Instructions Instructions
  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs, then add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (do not overmix).
  5. Pour batter into the preheated waffle iron and cook until golden brown and crisp.
  6. Serve warm with butter, syrup, fruits, or toppings of choice.
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place pie crust in a tart pan, trim edges, and prick the bottom with a fork.
  3. Blind bake the crust for 10 minutes using parchment paper and pie weights; remove weights and bake another 5 minutes.
  4. In a skillet, cook bacon until crisp; drain and crumble.
  5. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cream, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  6. Sprinkle cheese and bacon over the crust. Pour egg mixture on top.
  7. Bake for 35–40 minutes or until set and lightly golden. Cool slightly before slicing.
  1. To make bagels: Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water; let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in flour and salt to form a stiff dough. Knead for 10 minutes.
  3. Let dough rise for 1 hour until doubled, then divide into 8 portions and shape into bagels.
  4. Boil each bagel for 1–2 minutes per side in sweetened water.
  5. Place on a baking sheet, add toppings if desired, and bake at 425°F (220°C) for 20–25 minutes until golden.
  6. Cool slightly and slice. Spread with cream cheese and enjoy!
